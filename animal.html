Tiger

When a tiger wants to be heard, you’ll know about it, gang – because their roar can be heard as far as three kilometres away.

  • Scientific Name : Panthera tigris
  • Average Weight : 90-310
  • Average Speed : 49-65km/hr
  • Habitat : Evergreen forests

Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world. Adults can weigh up to 363kg – that’s about the same as ten ten year olds! – and measure up to 3.3m.Every tiger in the world is unique – no two tigers have the same pattern of stripes.
